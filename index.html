WHY TAO LINGO IS THE BEST

Our program was designed by top American teachers to make sure students learn the "lingo" not just the language. All language is art and we make the learning experience fun while also teaching the artistic expression so you sound like a native speaker. We have been teaching for over 20 years and have state of the art partnerships with Microsoft for a great learning experience.

We focus on the key aspects of linguistics to turn you not only into a speaker, but a master poet who has confidence in your communication skills.

OUR METHOD

TAO LEARNING APP

Powered by Tao Learning, we have an entire educational ecosystem that connects students with teachers and coursework. Tao Lingo is part of our P-20+ Initiative that provides learning solutions for students of all ages.

HAVE FUN WHILE LEARNING

Sing songs, laugh, and play with your teacher online as you build a lasting relationship with a teacher who will help you every step of the way. We trick the brain into learning with our unique style.

MICROSOFT EDUCATION PARTNERS

We are partnered with Microsoft for an amazing learning experience. You will receive access to a learning dashboard and interactive environment designed by the largest tech education company in the world.

LEARN THE LINGO

Speak just like an American. Learn the exact phrases, responses, and style of language used by American families. You will also learn from the same techniques that are taught by American families to their own children.
